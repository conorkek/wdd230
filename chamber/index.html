Manhattan Beach Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site will be named "Manhattan Beach Chamber of Commerce." It was selected because it represents the city where I am from and where the chamber operates and helps visitors immediately associate the site with the local business community.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to:

  • Provide local businesses and entrepreneurs with a platform to promote their services and connect with the community.
  • Act as a place for city information, economic development news, and business resources to help local commerce.

Scenarios

Common questions that visitors might have:

  • What events are available for local businesses this month?
  • Where can I find the chamber’s leadership team and contact information?
  • How can I register my business with the chamber?

Color Schema

Color scheme chosen for the site:

  • Primary Color: #003366 (Navy) – for headings and footer background.
  • Accent Color: #ffcc00 (Gold) – for buttons, links, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

Fonts selected for the site:

  • Headings: 'Roboto'
  • Body Text: 'Roboto'
